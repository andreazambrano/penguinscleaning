HEALTH AND MEDIC CENTERS

In Penguins Cleaning Service we count on four cleaning steps to get a complete hygiene anywhere in the hospital/clinic:

1. Cleaning:

Through the cleaning process it’s possible to eliminate all of the impurities and pick up the dirt what can there be on surfaces. We use basic products that remove any kind of dirt. This is our first step, but it is not enough to end with all of the germs and bacteria.

2.Disinfection:

In this step, we destroy the microorganisms what are able to spread through the surfaces, by using specific chemical products to reduce its virulence.

3. Sanitization:

We sanitize all the surfaces as a third step to you feel safe of be in a place where all of the hygiene requirements are accomplished.

4.Sterilization:

Our final step is to get a total and permanent destruction and elimination of microbial life. By using disinfectant substances, we get to remove all of the microorganisms.
